2008 - 2016 Institute of Technology Entrants

The above graph represents the number of New Entrants to Institutes of Technology from 2008 - 2016. The graph shows that there has a been a steady increase in the number of New Entrants to Institutes of Technology, with each year ranging between above 18.5K and less than 19.5K. In 2008 the number of entrants to Institutes of Technology was just 17.1K compared to 19.53K in 2015.

Top 5 Institutes of Technology in Ireland

DIT Cork IT Waterford IT Athlone IT IT Carlow

DIT Entrants

The above graph shows the number of New Entrants in Dublin Institute of Technology (DIT) from 2008 - 2016. DIT has remained the most popular choice of Institute of Technology in Ireland with a steady increase of New Entrants each year after a small decline in 2010. The number of New Entrants to DIT in 2010 was 2.91K compared to 3.73K in 2016. There are 20,000 students registered in DIT, and over 2000 members of staff. DIT will soon join forces with IT Tallaght and IT Blanchardstown to form a Technological University catering for the Greater Dublin Region and beyond. See DITs website for more information on Dublin Insitute of Technology and the development of the plans for a Technological University.

Cork IT Entrants

The above graph shows the number of New Entrants in Cork Institute of Technology (CIT) from 2008 - 2016. CIT currently has roughly 12,000 registered students of which 6,000 are full-time and the remaining are part-time. The part-time student population consists of a wide variety of students including access, evening and continued professional development students as well as a significant number studying music and drama at all levels. See Cork ITs website for more information.

Waterford IT Entrants

The above graph shows the number of New Entrants in Waterford Institute of Technology (WIT) from 2008 - 2016. Waterford Institute of Technology (WIT) is a university-level institution in the South-East of Ireland. Being the major provider of higher education in the South East region of Ireland, the Institute’s current full-time equivalent of students is in excess of 7,500 while part-time student enrolments exceed 2,500. WIT also provides employment to roughly 1000 members of staff. See Waterford ITs website for more information.

Athlone IT Entrants

The above graph shows the number of New Entrants in Athlone Institute of Technology (AIT) from 2008 - 2016. Athlone Institute of Technology (AIT) has been named Institute of Technology of the Year 2018. The Institute is home to 6,000 students undertaking a broad range of programmes in the domains of business, hospitality, engineering, informatics, science, health, social science and design. See Athlone ITs website for more information.

IT Carlow

The above graph shows the number of New Entrants in Institute of Technology Carlow (ITC) from 2008 - 2016. Founded in 1970 IT Carlow is located in the centre of Ireland’s South Leinster region. IT Carlow is the fourth largest Institute of Technology in Ireland with 7000 registered students. See IT Carlow's website for more information.
